About Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the Land of Kings, is India's largest state. It's famous for majestic forts and palaces, colorful festivals, and a rich craft heritage. From Jaipur’s pink palaces to Jaisalmer’s golden dunes, each city has its own story.

History in brief

Once a collection of Rajput kingdoms and princely states, Rajasthan's history is marked by valor, art patronage, and royal architecture. Forts like Amber and Mehrangarh stand as witnesses to centuries of culture and craftsmanship.

Culture & Traditions

Rajasthan boasts vibrant folk dances (Ghoomar, Kalbeliyya), soulful music (Sarangi, Dhol), and handicrafts like block-printing, blue pottery, and miniature paintings.

Heritage & Culture

Experience folk dances, royal traditions, and local flavors. Dive into crafts, festivals, and the living heritage that shapes Rajasthan.
